Day 1

June 9, 2020

Weighing in after a long weekend of food and drinks the scale read 185, the heaviest I have weighed all year. I read that it takes 90 days for something to become a habit so why not start now. My goal is to workout or run 6 days a week and to cut out all unhealthy snacks (with one cheat day).


The workout

To start off the week I ran a 5k at a time of 23:41 from the Nike Run app. It was a hot day and the run was tough, however I percerved and finished the run. Motivation is high at this point but it is only day 1.

Day 2

June 10, 2020

An evan hotter day at around 92 degrees I had the bright idea to go for a run at 12 o'clock. An easy 5 miles run (I thought) but it turned out to be a tough, sweaty and long run.


The workout

An "easy" 5 mile run to increase miles but not ruin the week. Ankles and calves were tight and sore for most the run, not sure if this was because of the hot weather or tiredness from Day 1's run. 5 miles was completed in 44:53 at 8'58 / mi.
